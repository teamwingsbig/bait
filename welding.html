Welding

Over the course of the past 25 years, we have grown our presence, knowledge and experience to master welding consumables, accessories and equipment. Through a collection of high quality products and tools from renowned suppliers such as Sakura, Sincosald and Techweld, we provide our clients with the opportunity to find their day-to-day needs met. Our application-based solutions cover the spectrum of process types, including SMAW, FCAW, GTAW, GMAW and SAW. We also procure a vast range of base metals such as carbon steel, stainless steel, duplex stainless steel, super duplex stainless steel and Inconel alloys, among others. Additionally, we offer a range of premium products such as welding machine, oven and high-rated accessories for arc and gas welding.
